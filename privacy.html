Mycomi Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25 June 2026

This Policy explains how Nasib Fahim Fernández (Spain) (the "Controller", "we") processes your personal data when you use the Mycomi app (the "App"), an internationally available mushroom identification and collection app. We comply with Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (GDPR) and the data protection laws applicable in your country (see section 10).

Data Controller: Nasib Fahim Fernández (Spain)
Contact / exercise your rights: mycomi.app@gmail.com

We do not collect email, password, phone, contacts or payment data (Premium purchases are processed by Google Play; we only learn whether your subscription is active). The App has no advertising and does not sell or share your data for commercial purposes. We use Firebase Crashlytics (Google) to record crashes and improve stability, and Firebase Analytics (Google) to understand app usage in aggregate; these may collect diagnostic data, usage events and device identifiers, processed by Google and transmitted encrypted; they are not used for personalized advertising and you can turn off usage analytics in Settings.

2. Where your data is stored

  • On your device (primary): profile, collection, photos and finds are stored locally.
  • Cloud sync (Google Firebase): the ranking/leaderboard is a Premium subscription feature; for Premium users, their name or alias, country, avatar and points are synced and shown publicly to other users within the App.
Important — data loss: because your collection and finds are stored locally, if you uninstall the App or delete your profile you will permanently and irrecoverably lose that data (collection, photos, points and progress). There is no backup or restore.

4. Who we share data with

  • Google (Firebase AI Logic / Gemini API, App Check): your photos and coordinates are sent to Google's AI only to perform the identification and obtain area information. Firebase Privacy · Google.
  • Wikimedia Foundation: the App requests reference images; it may log your IP. Wikimedia Privacy.
  • Google Play (Billing): processes Premium subscription purchases and confirms their status to us. We do not access your payment method.

We do not sell or disclose your data to third parties for advertising purposes.

5. International transfers

We operate globally. Your data may be processed on our providers' (Google) servers located outside your country or region, including the USA. These transfers rely on recognized mechanisms: EU Standard Contractual Clauses, the EU/UK/Swiss-US Data Privacy Framework, and/or your jurisdiction's equivalent standard clauses, ensuring an adequate level of protection.

6. Retention

  • Local data: until you delete it or uninstall the App.
  • Synced data (ranking): while you participate; deleting your profile removes it.
  • Photos sent to the AI: processed for identification; we do not keep them on a server of our own.

7. Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures: sensitive data stays local, requests travel encrypted (HTTPS) and are protected with App Check. No system is infallible, but we work to protect your information.

8. Children

The App is intended for people aged 13 or older. Where your country's law requires a higher age to consent on one's own behalf (e.g. 16 in parts of the EU, 14 in Spain, 16 in South Korea, etc.), minors need the verifiable consent of a parent or legal guardian. If we detect a minor's data without such consent, we will delete it.

9. Your rights (universal)

Wherever you live, you can ask us at mycomi.app@gmail.com for: access, rectification/correction, erasure, restriction, objection, portability and to withdraw consent (by revoking camera, location or notification permissions in your phone settings). You can exercise many of these yourself: you may delete your profile and collection from the App and revoke permissions from the system. We will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights.

GDPR, UK GDPR and the Swiss nFADP. You have the rights in section 9 and to lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority (in Spain, the AEPD; in the UK, the ICO; in Switzerland, the FDPIC).

🌎 Americas

USA (California — CCPA/CPRA, and Virginia, Colorado, Connecticut, Utah, Texas, etc.): right to know, access, correct, delete and to opt out of the "sale" or "sharing"; we do not sell or share your personal information. Right to non-discrimination.

Brazil (LGPD): rights of access, correction, anonymization, portability and deletion; authority: ANPD.
Mexico, Argentina, Chile, Colombia, Peru: ARCO rights (access, rectification, cancellation and objection) before the relevant national authority.

🌏 Asia and the Middle East

🌍 Africa

🌏 Oceania

Australia (Privacy Act / Australian Privacy Principles) and New Zealand (Privacy Act 2020): access and correction; complaint to the OAIC (Australia) or the Privacy Commissioner (New Zealand).

If your country is not listed, you can still exercise the universal rights in section 9 by contacting us.
